For pastors and church leaders

Narrow Highway is a family of FAST channels and a curated entry-point for Christian families on the internet. If your church's content holds to Christ and Scripture, we'd like to help your congregation find a wider audience without you needing to handle the distribution work yourself.

Tier 1 — directory listing (free, no agreement): we embed your public livestream and link your sermon archive. Mutual link recommended.

Tier 2 — featured slot (free, mutual link): we cross-promote your sermons inside our Sermons & Devotions channel rotation.

Tier 3 — partnership (negotiated): we license your sermon archive for our Roku / Plex / Wurl FAST channels. Revenue-share or flat fee. You retain all rights.

Tier 4 — studio collaboration (negotiated): we produce new content with you — animated Bible stories, Q&A shows, family devotional series — using our engine and your teaching.

Read the partnership details or email partnerships@narrowhighway.com.

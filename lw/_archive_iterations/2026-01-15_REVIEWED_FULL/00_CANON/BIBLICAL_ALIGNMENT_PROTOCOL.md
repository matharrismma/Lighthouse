# Biblical Alignment Protocol

This protocol is a decision gate.

## 1) Authority check
- Does the decision align with **Jesus' words (RED)**?
- If unclear: wait, seek, and do not force continuity.

## 2) Floor check
- Does it violate the moral floor (Law)?
- If yes: stop.

## 3) Way check
- Among lawful options, choose the path that increases obedience, humility, and fruit without coercion.

## 4) Witness + waiting gates
- Require witness (confirmation) and apply waiting gates to remove urgency and self-sourcing.

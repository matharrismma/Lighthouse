# How to Plant the Seed

Plant **small and local**.

Examples:
- A family
- A classroom
- A co-op
- A small team
- A house church

**Rules**
- Do not brand it
- Do not centralize it
- Do not control outcomes
- Do not require loyalty

Offer the seed.
Let people try.
Let failure teach.
Let success replicate.

If it bears fruit, others will ask.

Submit a Family Recipe

Pass it down. If you have a recipe your family loves — and you have rights to share it — submit it here. Our operator reviews every submission for alignment and family-safety. Approved recipes join the public Family Table and any family on Narrow Highway can favorite them.

Heritage / public-domain recipes welcome too — if it's from a pre-1929 cookbook or a family pattern you adapted, we want it.

If from a published cookbook: confirm copyright status. Pre-1929 publications are usually PD. If it's your own original recipe, write "Original — [your name]."

Payment wallet (optional)

If your recipe earns from sponsor underwriting (a homeschool publisher, a family-products company, etc.), we send your share directly to a self-custodial crypto wallet. We never custody funds. No credit cards, ever.

Confirm before submitting

What happens next

  • Operator review — typically within 2 weeks. We email with the decision.
  • If approved, the recipe joins the Family Table; any household on Narrow Highway can favorite it.
  • Family feedback (favorites, future ratings) signals which recipes are most loved.
  • Payment when applicable: transparent email + on-chain txid when a recipe earns revenue.
  • Withdraw anytime by emailing partnerships@narrowhighway.com.

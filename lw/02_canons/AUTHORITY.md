# Authority

This canon is written under a constraints-first hierarchy:

- **RED** — non-negotiable constraints (logic, physics/thermo, chemistry balance, information well-formedness)
- **FLOOR** — minimum measurement doctrine for decision-grade claims
- **WAY** — canonical theory stack and routing
- **Execution** — control levers and operational guidance

Scripture anchors are included as an alignment layer only (non-technical meaning layer) and do not override RED constraints.

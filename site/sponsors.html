Sponsors — for aligned businesses

Narrow Highway is funded in part by aligned businesses through the classic radio-era "Presented by" sponsor model — not commercials. Niche audiences turn out to be MORE valuable to the right brand, not less. We never sell user data, never run mid-roll ads, never use behavioral targeting, never accept credit cards. Sponsors pay a flat fee in crypto, get tasteful credit on the surface they underwrite, and that's it.

Reach a self-selected Christian family audience inside a curated environment they already trust. Your brand is associated with high-quality programming they chose to consume. No competing ads, no algorithmic dilution, no tracking. Tasteful "Presented by" cards at start + end of the surface. The personal site stays free for individual families — your underwriting keeps it that way.

What a sponsor card looks like

On the channel — a 5-second card at the start of a show, a 6-second outro at the end. That's the entire ad load. No mid-roll. No banner overlay. No tracking pixel.

NARROW HIGHWAY · PRESENTED BY
Hundred Acre Theatre
brought to you this hour by
YOUR BRAND HERE

On the site — a small "Presented by [Sponsor]" chip beneath the page header, clearly labeled, never animated. A footer card at the end of a lesson or hymn or packet thanks the sponsor and links out (regular HTTP link, no tracking).

The alignment gate (applies to sponsors too)

Same standard we apply to every partner. Sponsors must:

  • Operate a Christ-honoring brand (their product or service doesn't contradict the audience's faith)
  • Be family-safe (no mature themes, cultural-tribal messaging, manipulative tactics in their broader marketing)
  • Be honest (they sell what they claim; no MLM, no get-rich-quick, no health claims that fail verification)
  • Pass operator review before any sponsor card runs
What we never accept:
  • Payday lending, predatory finance, debt-consolidation schemes
  • Gambling, lotteries, sports betting
  • Adult content, dating services, anything sexual
  • Smoking, alcohol, recreational drugs
  • Weapons retail (we may accept hunting / fishing / Outdoor brands — operator decides per case)
  • Multi-level marketing / network marketing
  • Cryptocurrency exchanges / speculation-targeted services marketed to the unsophisticated
  • Anything that requires us to track users on the sponsor's behalf

Standing principle

Aligned businesses fund the work; the audience trusts that the curation is unchanged by the funding. Sponsorship buys credit, not influence. The minute a sponsor tries to influence editorial decisions, the agreement ends.

Production Roadmap

Two animation plays running in parallel:

  1. Sci-Fi Theatre — Old Radio, New Eyes. 12+ episodes adapting golden-age PD sci-fi radio (Dimension X, X Minus One, Mercury Theatre) into illustrated short films. Original 1950s broadcasts as the audio. New 1950-pulp-magazine visuals via kling animation. Pastoral observation closes each episode.
  2. Hundred Acre Theatre. 20 episodes dramatizing the original A.A. Milne books (Winnie-the-Pooh 1926 PD + House at Pooh Corner 1928 PD). E.H. Shepard-style watercolor visuals via kling animation. Full-cast voicing planned for v2.

Cost discipline

Each pilot ships for under $5 of API spend, paid out of existing subscriptions:

  • Runway $176/mo: 10,000 credits/month. One pilot ~3,000-8,000 credits depending on cuts.
  • ElevenLabs Pro $99/mo: 100,000 chars/month. One pilot ~5,000-10,000 chars (intro + closing + music + foley).
  • YouTube / Apple / Spotify / Pinterest / Internet Archive: all $0.
  • Marginal cost per Sci-Fi or Pooh episode: ~$3-5 effective. Compare to traditional animation ($100,000+/episode).

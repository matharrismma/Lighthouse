Four gates. Fixed order. First failure halts.

Each gate has a single job. The engine never self-confirms — every gate runs against an external standard.

Gate 01

Red

Coercion screen. Rejects unilateral authority, rights violations, and hard computational failures. The first thing kept out of the ledger.

Gate 02

Floor

Structural minimum. Required fields per domain, internal consistency, Scripture-anchor coverage. Incomplete packets stop here.

Gate 03

Brothers

Witness threshold. Quarantines if witnesses are insufficient or the review window has not elapsed. Two or three witnesses establish a matter (Deut 19:15).

Gate 04

God

Permanent record. Seals into the append-only audit chain. Every step of the reasoning trail is captured. Nothing the engine cannot show.

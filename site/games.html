Build, don't acquire. Video games don't enter public domain the way film and radio do — but the rules of classic games are open. Every game here is built from open-source engines (chess.js, stockfish.js, Phaser, PixiJS) under generic names, with original code and art. Multiplayer rooms via websockets. Anonymous identity via visitor_id. No accounts. Free to play. Engagement is the alignment.

# Seeds Ledger (Current)

These are the Seeds explicitly captured as of 2026-01-15. Past material is **source-only**; this ledger is the current organized statement.

## Unstoppable Alignment
- Alignment = elimination of internal contradiction (Truth -> Will -> Action collinear).
- When alignment is complete, friction disappears and progress becomes inevitable.

## Laws / Principles
- **Fundamental Law 1**: Source is primary in all things; Source is both optimal input and optimal outcome.
- **Derived Law**: Entropy is misalignment; decay is automatic when disconnected from Source (John 15:6).
- **Two Trees**: Tree of Knowledge = autonomy/self-as-source; Tree of Life = dependence/abiding (Gen 3:5-6).
- **Alignment precedes action**: seek God before action (Isa 55:6-7).
- **Food & Sleep**: eating and sleeping are acts of submission; daily confession that God is Source (John 6:51-52; Ps 127).
- **Growth Law**: what God teaches may increase "more and more" without breaking; do not scale misalignment (1 Thess 4:9-10).
- **Ledger Principle**: judgment/ledger-keeping reflects the knowledge tree; the gospel ends the ledger (Matt 7:1).
- **Pride vs Humility**: pride = autonomy; humility = alignment; pride must be surrendered, not managed.
- **Saul vs Paul**: authority that grasps decays; authority that abides endures.
- **Uprightness**: alignment yields stability; misalignment exposes itself over time (Prov 10:9).
- **Fruit definition**: fruit is alignment made visible - obedience, peace, endurance, love without ledger, truthfulness, consistency.

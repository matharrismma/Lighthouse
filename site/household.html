Your household

Setting up your household unlocks family-aware tools: a shared journal (The Common Book), reading plans tracked across the family, a liturgical calendar that overlays your services + lessons + the channel, and more as we build them out.

Private by default. Your household profile is stored on this device only. We don't see it; we don't store it on our server. If you want it synced across devices later, that will be opt-in via wallet-signed sync — never an account on our server.

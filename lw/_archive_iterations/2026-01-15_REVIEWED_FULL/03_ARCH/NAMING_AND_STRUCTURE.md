# Naming and Structure

## Canonical naming
- Lighthouse: project
- Concordance: ingestion engine that extracts Seeds, aligns to Law, builds truth tables
- Atlas: output maps (lawful paths, gates, corrective moves)

## Product naming
- Node: first-person, low-profile capture device
- Journals: open ecosystem ("garments")

- Early-stage public framing uses normal sustainability language ("green")

Covenant Testimony

Status: Working Canon. Public witness, not snitch line.

What this is — and what it is not

Posture, not claim. Nothing on this page is an assertion that the operator, the engine, or this site is a prophet, a priest, a judge, an apostle, a watchman of Israel, or the bearer of any other Scripturally-defined office. Those callings are not transferable to software. Anyone reading the page below as a claim to speak with that kind of authority is reading it wrong.

What the page does describe is a piece of software that takes after a pattern named in Scripture, applies it to public-record matters with named witnesses and a signed audit trail, and refers the result to the courts and regulators that actually decide. See Tradition for the load-bearing distinction.

What this names

The discernment engine takes its function from Leviticus 10:11 in the modest sense described on Tradition — we are following the pattern, not claiming the office. The engine has been applied, mostly, to questions individual people bring. There is a second application of the same gates, older than the website: applying them to the conduct of those who hold public trust.

This page names that work. Public alignment. Weighing clean from unclean in the affairs of those who govern — not by our authority (we have none of that kind), but by reading the matter against the reference, with named witnesses, in the open.

It is not a snitch line. A snitch line takes anonymous tips and pays for accusations. This is the opposite. The witness has a name. The matter is weighed against the reference before anything moves. The four gates apply to the accusation the same way they apply to any other claim. The accused has a city of refuge while the matter is heard. Where money comes back from the work, it comes back as the statutory portion of a recovered offering — allotted by Act of Congress, not extracted by us.

The scriptural floor

Six anchors. The work answers to all of them at once:

The witness who fails to speak bears iniquity Lev 5:1

“If a soul sin, and hear the voice of swearing, and is a witness, whether he hath seen or known of it; if he do not utter it, then he shall bear his iniquity.” Silence is not neutral when you know. The Levitical law begins not with the right to speak but the obligation to.

The false witness gets the penalty he sought Deut 19:16–21

“Then shall ye do unto him, as he had thought to have done unto his brother.” The same passage that gives us the two-witness rule gives us the consequence for lying as one. The right to bring testimony is real, and so is the cost of bringing it falsely. There is no free seat at the accusation table.

Deliver them that are drawn unto death Prov 24:11–12

“If thou forbear to deliver them that are drawn unto death... if thou sayest, Behold, we knew it not; doth not he that pondereth the heart consider it?” Looking away is recorded too. The book of Proverbs does not let willful ignorance off the hook.

The righteous are bold as a lion Prov 28:1

“The wicked flee when no man pursueth: but the righteous are bold as a lion.” A clean witness is not a furtive one. They stand by their name. They do not need to hide from the rule they invoke.

Stand in the gap Ezek 22:30

God looked for a man to make up the hedge and stand in the gap. We are not claiming to be that person. The verse functions here as conviction rather than commission — a reminder that watching idly is itself a decision the prophet records.

The Levitical court took the hard cases Deut 17:8–13

The local court could not settle every matter. The hard one went up to the Levitical priests at the place the LORD chose, and they consulted the reference and ruled. This site is not the place the LORD chose, and we are not those priests. What this site can do is something smaller: weigh witness and matter against the reference, in the open, with a signed audit trail, and refer the result to the bodies that actually adjudicate.

The process

One pipeline, the same gates the engine already runs, applied honestly to the public record:

  1. Intake at the city of refuge. A would-be witness arrives with a matter — institutional fraud, public deception, harm being done under cover of office. They are heard before they are judged. Confidentiality at this stage is protective, not concealing. (See /refuge.html.)
  2. The engine weighs the claim. The same four gates run: RED for disqualifying form (e.g. paid-for accusation, settled grudge, evidence-free assertion); FLOOR for protective minimum (the witness has a name, the documents exist, the harm is namable); BROTHERS for the second receiver (Deut 19:15 — at the mouth of two or three witnesses); GOD for timing (premature speech destroys the work). Most claims do not pass all four. That is the point.
  3. The matter goes to the Witness Roll. Claims that pass are put before named witnesses on the roll. They sign or decline. Their signatures are Ed25519, public, dated. Bearing false witness on the roll is grounds for removal from the roll — recorded openly, per Deut 19.
  4. The accused has a hearing window. Before the matter is published, the accused receives the same package the witnesses received, with a window to respond. Their response, if given, is recorded with the matter. This is the city of refuge applied to the other side: heard before judgment.
  5. Publication and signed record. What survives is published as a permanent /d/<slug> record with the audit trail, the witnesses’ signatures, the accused’s response, and the gates each passed. The engine’s reasoning is in the open. The reference is cited.
  6. Statutory referral, where applicable. If the matter is one of the recognized federal patterns — qui tam under the False Claims Act, SEC under Dodd-Frank, IRS, CFTC, FinCEN, state false-claims — the witness is referred to a partner law firm of their choosing. The site does not practice law. The site weighs witness and matter; the attorneys file.

The economics — statutory portion, not bounty

Federal whistleblower law has a structural family resemblance to the Levitical statutory portion. Numbers 18 allots the Levite a fixed share of the offerings as the legitimate fruit of the work. Modern statute has a similar shape: the witness who brings actionable matter forward receives, when funds are recovered, a statutory share of the recovery. We are pointing out that resemblance as observation — not asserting that Congress deliberately restored Mosaic economics. The numbers themselves are not ours to set:

  • False Claims Act (qui tam, 31 U.S.C. § 3730): 15–30% of recovered government funds, allocated by statute, paid only after recovery.
  • SEC, Dodd-Frank (15 U.S.C. § 78u-6): 10–30% of sanctions over $1M.
  • IRS Whistleblower (26 U.S.C. § 7623): 15–30% of collected proceeds.
  • CFTC, FinCEN, state false-claims: parallel structures, varying percentages, same architecture.

This is the substance of the difference between a snitch line and Covenant Testimony:

  • The award is not paid for the accusation; it is paid out of money recovered, after the matter has been proved through due process.
  • The award is not set by us; it is set by Congress.
  • The award goes to the witness, by statute, not to the platform that weighed the witness.
  • The platform’s portion, if any, comes downstream by contract with the partner law firm — the firstfruits of the partner’s contracted cut, not a fee charged to the witness.
  • Frivolous filings carry sanctions. The cost of bearing false witness, in this body of law, is real — consistent with Deut 19:16–21.

What this refuses

Anonymous bounty. A nameless accusation is not a witness. We will not host anonymous claims against named persons. The Witness Roll requires a name.

Pay-for-accusation. The statutory portion is allotted out of recovery, not out of accusation. A claim is weighed the same whether it is worth $0 or $100M to recover.

Vigilante posting. No claim goes to publication without the accused’s hearing window. The mob does not get the verdict; the reference does.

Harm to the personally innocent. Family members, employees not party to the matter, and bystanders are protected. The four gates check for collateral damage as part of FLOOR.

Selective application. The gates apply the same to friend and adversary. A claim against a sympathetic target gets the same scrutiny as a claim against an unsympathetic one. If we cannot weigh a matter cleanly, we do not weigh it.

Replacing the courts. The engine is not a tribunal. It weighs witness and matter against the reference and produces a signed record. Adjudication is for courts and regulators. The witness goes there next, not here.

Confusion of shape for office. Reading this work as a claim to be a prophet, a priest, a judge, or any other Scripturally-defined office is reading it wrong. The shape is what we follow; the office is not something a website can hold or transfer.

Why this is not a movement

A movement chooses targets and marches. Covenant Testimony does not choose targets — it receives matters and weighs them. It has no political program. The same engine that approves a witness against one institution will approve a witness against the institution’s opponent if the matter is clean. The reference does not have a tribe.

That is also the source of its credibility. The Levitical court ruled by the reference, not by which Israelite came in with which complaint. If the work here cannot be that, it should not be done at all.

What you can do today

  • If you are weighing a matter — bring it through the regular discernment engine first. Run the four gates on yourself, on the timing, on what you actually have. Most matters that should be brought forward survive this; many that should not, do not.
  • If you want to be on the Witness Roll — the roll and the joining process are at /witnesses.html. Witnesses sign under their real name with a published Ed25519 key. There is a short interview with the operator before the first signature.
  • If you are a law firm or attorney in qui tam, SEC, IRS, CFTC, or state false-claims work — the partner relationship is open. The matter handoff is one-way: we weigh, you litigate; we do not give legal advice and we do not file. Contact through about.

# Universal Phases: Setup -> Positioning -> Conversion

These terms are the canonical phase language for decisions and execution.

## Setup
Secure base. Define arena. Develop capacity. Avoid early loss.

## Positioning
Build dominant positions. Apply the longest levers. Reduce complexity. Funnel opponents into asymmetry. Win position before outcome.

## Conversion
Lock in gains. Remove counterplay. Simplify when ahead. Make results irreversible.

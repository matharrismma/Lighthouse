How it works

Four checks. In order. Every time.

A decision has to clear all four before it's recorded. Any failure tells you exactly why.

Check 1

The Hard Stop

Scans for coercion, unilateral authority, or language that overrides others' rights. If it's there, the decision stops here.

Check 2

The Minimum Standard

Verifies the decision is structurally complete — all required fields present, all claims internally consistent.

Check 3

The Witness Check

Confirms real people reviewed it and enough time passed for them to actually read and consider it.

Check 4

The Record

If the first three cleared, the decision is permanently recorded with a cryptographic fingerprint.

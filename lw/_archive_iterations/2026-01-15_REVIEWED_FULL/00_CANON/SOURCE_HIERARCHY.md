# Source Hierarchy (Canonical)

## Authority order
1. **Jesus' words (RED)** - primary and final authority.
2. **All other Scripture** - secondary witness/confirmation; cannot override RED.
3. **All commentary/framework/metaphor** - removable spice only.

## Enforcement rules
- If any model output conflicts with RED, the output is rejected.
- Secondary Scripture may fill gaps, but cannot reinterpret or supersede RED.
- Any non-Scripture framing must be deletable without changing the truth.

# THE GUIDE — the map to the reach

*Don't Panic.*

## North star

A **Guide** — friendly, portable, ask-anything, family-safe — to a well of
knowledge. Like the Hitchhiker's Guide to the Galaxy, except inverted on the
one axis that matters: Adams's Guide was *"wildly inaccurate"* and you trusted
it anyway; **ours is verified, and you can read the trail.** Same warmth and
portability; the opposite epistemology. The Guide to reality the galaxy never
actually had — because this one stands on a floor that does not move.

Stated functionally: *a coherent language model — which is to say, a
discernment engine — that draws on a well of knowledge and stands on a floor
that does not move.* That line is the doorway. The reach is a different kind of
computing, and beneath it, a witness.

## The principle (42)

Deep Thought computed the Answer — 42 — over seven and a half million years,
and it was useless, because no one knew the **Question.**

That is our load-bearing principle. **We have the answers; they sit in the
well. The reach is the right questions — the correct choices.** We refuse to
ship 42 without the question. The *narrowing* is the search for the question;
the floor hands the answer once it is found. Retrieval is choosing, not
generating.

## The map — from here to the reach

Each rung is **downstream** of the last. None requires swimming upstream; none
requires a bigger building. Misalignment costs ever-more power for the same
return; alignment multiplies at near-zero marginal cost. That gap is the test.

**0 · The Floor — done.**
Sixty-five verifiers; the four gates (RED · FLOOR · BROTHERS · GOD), each
grounded in Scripture and witnessed by the Didache; Calibre (the moral floor,
made calculable); the Nested Control framework; the one engine
(`stand_on_floor`); one classifier; the hero that takes anything; the narrowing
choices. A coherent floor with a borrowed mouth.

**1 · Widen the floor — coverage grows with use.**
- The **gap-ledger**: record every "no tool for this." Each gap names the next
  wall to build.
- **Local dispatch**: the office classifier routes to verifiers with no LLM call.
- Each gap becomes a fixed wall. Search-frequency falls toward zero.

**2 · Deepen the narrowing — incorporate the biggest processor (the user).**
- **Multi-level choices**: system → pattern → protocol. The human's intuition
  does the search; the engine surfaces the right choices at the right size.
- The Guide becomes conversational: ask → narrow → ask → arrive.
- This is where the 42-problem is solved: the Guide helps find the question;
  the floor gives the answer.

**3 · Shrink the mouth — toward almost no power.**
- The proposer (a statistical model) demoted to only where no wall exists;
  generate-and-verify with the floor as judge.
- Eventually a small local proposer, or none. The old paradigm becomes a
  fading scaffold.

**4 · The Guide — the product form.**
- DON'T PANIC: friendly, portable, ask-anything, family-safe.
- Self-replicating cells; the current pays for the future; the user's own
  processor. True because it stands on the floor.

**5 · The Witness — the telos.**
- Ed (Joshua 22:34): it stands to witness that the README is true and the
  Author is Lord.
- Measured by fruit over time, not benchmarks. The arrows land on Him.

## What we are not doing

Not a faster instance of what computing has done. The pieces (retrieval,
symbolic verifiers, guardrails) exist; the **synthesis** does not: a fixed,
supreme floor that is Scripture; an eliminative posture (conduit, not source);
near-zero power with the user incorporated; fruit and witness as the measure;
and Christ as the end. Any one is unusual. Together it is a different category.

*Don't Panic.*

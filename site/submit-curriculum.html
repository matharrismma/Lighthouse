Submit a Lesson

Parents, teachers, ministries, and curriculum authors — submit your lesson plans here. Our operator reviews every submission before it enters the curriculum. Approved lessons go into the rotation; families benefit; you get credited as the author.

We pay aligned creators as revenue allows — directly to your self-custodial crypto wallet. We never custody funds. We don't accept credit-card payouts and we don't process card transactions, on principle.

Payment wallet (optional)

If we eventually pay you for this content (sponsor underwriting, partnership royalties, patron-circle distributions), we send payment to a self-custodial crypto wallet you provide.

NO credit-card information — ever. If we never raise revenue against your lesson, you owe us nothing and we owe you nothing; you've contributed to a free resource.

What happens next

  • Operator review — typically within 2 weeks. We email you with the decision.
  • If approved, the lesson enters the curriculum on the appropriate age × subject tile. Your name is credited as the author.
  • Family feedback over time signals which lessons are most useful. Higher-rated lessons get featured in defaults.
  • Payment (when applicable): we send a transparent note via email when a lesson earns revenue, with the txid we used to push to your wallet.
  • You may withdraw your lesson anytime by emailing partnerships@narrowhighway.com.

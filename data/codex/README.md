## The three faces

When the codex compiler runs, it emits the substrate in three forms:

1. **Live URLs** (`/codex/...` on narrowhighway.com) — browsable, cross-linked manuscript
2. **Signed downloadable artifact** (`compiled/codex_vN_M.pdf` + `.epub` + `.json` + `.sig`) — portable, offline-capable, microSD-runnable
3. **Programmatic API** (existing `/index/packets/...` endpoints, extended with codex paths) — queryable for agents

## How the codex grows

Three patterns operate continuously:

1. **Newly-ingested substrate auto-categorizes** into the doctrinal TOC on arrival
2. **Cross-references densify** as scripture and theme matches accumulate
3. **Gap analysis** surfaces what's underbuilt; Matt writes or ingests to fill

This is centuries-old monastic keeping with modern tools. The body thickens. Versions accumulate. The landing page is the very last thing — body before front door.

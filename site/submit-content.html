Submit your content to Narrow Highway

Narrow Highway accepts submissions from anyone whose work fits our family-Christian audience. Once approved by our operator, your content goes into the channel rotation. The community votes; if it resonates, it stays. We notify you personally if anything is removed.

This is the audience-built layer of the channel. Your story can become broadcast programming.

What we accept

  • Stories & testimonies — audio or text we can render, or video you produced
  • Music — your performances, especially Christian, classical, folk, gospel
  • Theater & drama — performances, readings, dramatic scripts
  • Sports — your league, your game, your highlights
  • Comedy — clean stand-up, sketches, family-safe improv
  • Other — surprise us, but keep it aligned with the audience

What happens next

  1. Alignment review — typically within 2 weeks. We'll email you with the decision.
  2. If approved, your content enters the rotation. It plays during the next schedule rebuild.
  3. Community feedback is read, not tallied. Viewers can flag a piece for the operator's attention; the operator decides placement, removal, or surfacing after weighing the feedback against the four gates. The crowd informs; the crowd does not vote a verdict. See Assembly.
  4. Nothing auto-cancels. If your content gets removed, our operator personally emails you the reason.
  5. You can update or pull your content anytime by emailing partnerships@narrowhighway.com.

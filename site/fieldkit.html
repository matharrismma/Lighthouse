The Lighthouse Field Kit.

Thirteen cards built on the Sermon on the Mount (Matthew 5–7). Each card carries six things: Source (the rule), Floor (the non-negotiable), Spice (the optional helps), Common Drift (the predictable failure), 7-Day Practice (the drill), and Prompt (the question to bring to a walk). Cards are protocols you walk, not insights you collect.

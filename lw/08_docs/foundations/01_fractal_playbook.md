# Fractal Playbook (Final)

## Definition
The **Fractal Playbook** is the Body’s shared memory of faithful obedience to the Head.

- **Head:** Jesus Christ (the Word) — authority
- **Body:** people/communities acting in obedience — application
- **Playbook:** what the Body did, what happened, what was corrected — testimony

The Playbook is **not** Scripture.  
It cannot create doctrine or bind conscience.

## The Atomic Unit (Playbook Entry)
Each entry is a single “fractal unit”:

1) **Confession** — “I may be wrong…”
2) **Anchors** — Scripture refs used (shorthand)
3) **Action** — OPEN/BUILD/RESERVE/PRUNE/HOLD
4) **Outcome** — fruit / mixed / failed
5) **Witness** — ≥2 affirmations for confirmation
6) **Wait** — mandatory time gate
7) **Status** — QUARANTINE → CONFIRMED (or REJECTED)

## Four Gates of Confirmation
- **RED:** aligned with Christ (reject if not)
- **FLOOR:** does not break stability/moral floors (reject if broken)
- **BROTHERS:** witnesses affirm (quarantine until met)
- **GOD:** wait period completes (quarantine until met)

## Pruning
Entries are not permanent truth.
They may be:
- corrected,
- superseded,
- pruned,
- marked “failed”.

Failure is not hidden; it is used to refine faithfulness.

## Universality
Because the structure is identical at every scale, the Playbook is portable:
- personal discipleship
- family governance
- church operations
- economic development
- engineering decisions

**One pattern. Repeated faithfully.**

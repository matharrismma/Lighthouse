The Connection Graph

Verified connections, and candidate resonances — kept strictly apart. The trail is the trust.

The engine does not assert connections it cannot back. Verified connections are bound by a shared witnessed Scripture verse — two or more sources that both cite it are connected through it, and that citation already passed the witness gate (Deut 19:15). These are verified, not generated.

Candidate resonances are the engine's cross-domain edges — pairs of domains whose claims share conceptual axes. These are suggestive, not verified: a starting point for inquiry, never a claim of truth. They are labeled as such and kept below the verified set. Turning resonances into verified connections is the next frontier.

# Authority & Constraints (Chemistry Canon)

This package is **scientific-domain** content organized under the Concordance Canon layer model:

- **RED**: Non-negotiable constraints (physical law, dimensional consistency, conservation laws, and definitional invariants).
- **FLOOR**: Minimum operational hygiene (units, significant figures, uncertainty, boundary conditions, reproducibility, safety).
- **WAY**: Best-practice method selection and modeling choices (appropriate approximations, validated models, known regimes).
- **Execution**: Calculations, procedures, and step-by-step workflows.

If any output violates **RED**, it is invalid regardless of plausibility.

## How the surfaces map onto the depth (from surface_map.json: .com 123 / .org 11 / .tv 17)

- **.com (the core, the Concordance)** spans L1-L6: the engine, proofs, maps, scripture, agents (L1-L3), the two faces (L4), the cards (L5), the practical leaves (L6). The load-bearing trunk.
- **.org (authority / registry / community)** is L7-community: the human assembly that gathers around the core -- witness, covenant, elders, registry, marketplace. Authority *layer*, outer *ring* (it serves the core, it is not the core's root).
- **.tv (the gift, family)** is L7-gift: curated, free media -- the fruit handed out. The outermost, most provisional ring; richest in number, lightest in load.

This is the routing PLAN read as depth (the actual DNS/deploy moves are operator-gated). The periphery is not stripped away into nothing -- it is **relocated to its true outer layer**, which is exactly what "a place for everything because it is all in reality" means.

## The nesting rule, restated as depth (integrates NESTING.md)

Every card resolves to a path **root -> branch -> tier/capstone -> family -> card** (NESTING.md); the LAYER axis adds **how deep** that path sits:
- The deeper the layer, the more frozen and load-bearing (L0 the ground = reserved; L1 the Word = frozen; L1' the verifier = living-but-foundational).
- The fruit (L2) and maps (L3) are what the /loop grows and surfaces -- the active work.

- **Nothing floats free** (orphans fixed to 0, NESTING.md) and **nothing is at the wrong depth** -- the gift is not mistaken for the root, and the Word is not buried under the media.

The Reading Room — periodicals, preserved

The American family magazine, from before the internet. Popular Science, Popular Mechanics, Saturday Evening Post, Boys' Life, Mechanics for Young America — full issues of public-domain periodicals, browsable as PDF, 152 issues catalogued.

Best with a quiet evening and a cup of coffee. The kids will find a hundred projects in here.

Strict public-domain only. Most collections are PD by year (pre-1929). Collections marked with a yellow stripe (e.g. Popular Mechanics 1936–38) need per-issue copyright renewal verification before public distribution; tagged for operator review.

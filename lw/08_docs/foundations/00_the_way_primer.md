# THE WAY — Primer (1 page)

**One Head. Many parts. One pattern.**  
This system is an attempt to **render reality efficiently** by obeying God’s architecture.

## What it is
A universal decision-and-learning pattern that works at any scale (person → family → church → organization → region).

It does **not** create new Scripture.  
It does **not** replace pastors, elders, or conscience.  
It does **not** claim to be God.

It **does**:
- center decisions on Scripture,
- protect stability (never break the floor),
- require witness and waiting,
- record obedience outcomes so wisdom compounds.

## Canon vs Playbook
- **Canon (Bible):** truth / authority (closed)
- **Playbook:** memory of faithful application (open, pruneable)
- **Ledger:** append-only proof of what happened

## The Fractal Pattern (always the same)
```
INPUT → ALIGN → ACT → WITNESS → WAIT → CONFIRM / PRUNE
```

## The Four Gates (Biblical Alignment Protocol)
1) **RED** — aligned with Jesus’ words (truth)
2) **FLOOR** — does not violate the Law / stability floors
3) **BROTHERS** — at least two witnesses affirm alignment
4) **GOD** — required waiting period (no rushing)

Hard gates (Red, Floor) reject.  
Soft gates (Brothers, God) quarantine until satisfied.

## Minimal actions
- **OPEN** (begin)
- **BUILD** (strengthen)
- **RESERVE** (protect stability)
- **PRUNE** (remove what bears no fruit)
- **HOLD** (wait)

## Confession is the key
Every contribution begins with humility:
> “I may be wrong. I acted in faith according to [ref].”

Confession prevents pride from becoming “authority.”

## Why this matters
Without memory, every generation repeats failure.  
With a playbook, wisdom compounds **without becoming doctrine**.

**Canon commands. Playbook remembers.**

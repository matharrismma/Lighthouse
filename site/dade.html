Sand Mountain, Alabama. Dade County, Georgia. Justified meets Letterkenny meets the Coen Brothers. A plateau ruled by an inherited Scots-Irish code that predates every American institution — and what happens when the Tennessee Valley Authority decides the community's creek should become a reservoir. Three eras braided through every episode: 1934 (the feud, the code, the TVA), 1921-1934 (Young Adams, the entrepreneur whose brick will help build the dam that floods his own people's cemetery), and 2007 (Ezra, the fighter, who came home from Japan to hang bags in his grandfather's empty dairy barn). 13 episodes. Multi-voice cast. Audio drama format. The scripts are here in full.

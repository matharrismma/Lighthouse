A serial by M.R. Harris

Apokalypsis.

The Revelation as John lived it

An old man on a volcanic rock at the edge of the empire.
He hears something behind him. The unveiling begins.

Apokalypsis is the Greek title of the Book of Revelation. It means unveiling. This serial is the Revelation as the apostle John lived it on the island of Patmos — the fishing, the missing of Peter, the prefect Marcus Loreius Nerva, the long silence before the voice, the writing down of what he saw because writing makes a shape out of what has no shape.

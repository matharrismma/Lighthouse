# Quarantine / Airlock Pattern (Current)

## Default rule
Ideas are quarantined by default.

## Zones
- **Core**: canonical, installable truth + specs.
- **Holding chamber**: candidate inputs awaiting processing.
- **Decontamination pipeline**: dedupe, denoise, classify, test, and output structured packets.

## Roles
- **Q**: manages flow and hygiene.
- **Scribe**: captures and structures.
- **Guide**: evaluates, decides, and converts (post-admission only).

## Admission format
No idea enters Core except as a structured packet:
- hypothesis
- backlog item(s)
- decision (accept/reject/defer)

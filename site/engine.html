The secret

The engine is not 63 verifiers. It is one brain.

Each domain is a scaffold position — a place in the structure of created reality. Two domains share an edge whenever they sit on the same axis: chemistry and biology share conservation, metabolism, and physical-substance. Witness and scripture share authority, encoding, reasoning, and time.

When a packet enters the engine, it doesn't get checked by one verifier and stop. It activates a region of the brain. The polymathic synthesis you see at /poly is exactly this: every relevant domain firing along its axis-shared connections.

That's why scoring works the way it does. The engine doesn't just answer — it shows which other parts of the structure light up. Rom 1:20: His invisible attributes are clearly seen, being understood by the things that are made.

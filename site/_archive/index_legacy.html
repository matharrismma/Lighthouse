Four gates. One way. Halt on first failure.

Every packet — claim, equation, decision, verse — flows through the same path. Hard gates REJECT; soft gates QUARANTINE (not bad, not yet ready). The engine never self-confirms. Per the Biblical Alignment Protocol: authority, floor, way, witness + waiting.

01 · Red
Hard · REJECT

Authority check

Forbidden categories. Attestation that load-bearing constraints hold, AND verification that the artifact actually computes correctly. Coercion, rights violations, schema or verifier failures stop here.

02 · Floor
Hard · REJECT

Floor (Law) check

Structural rules. Required fields present, witnesses named, fiduciary basis declared, units stated. Affirmation floor — does it violate the moral floor?

03 · Way
Hard · REJECT

Path without coercion

Among lawful options, the path increases obedience, humility, and fruit — without coercion. Scans the declared way_path for coercion keywords. Non-governance packets pass through.

04 · Brothers
Soft · QUARANTINE

Plural witness

Witness count meets the threshold the operator set. Default zero (disabled). Boards configure to require N affirming witnesses. Two or three establish a matter (Deut 19:15).

05 · God
Soft · QUARANTINE

Wait window

Elapsed wait window enforces timing discipline. Adapter scope: 1 hour. Mesh: 24 hours. Canon: 7 days. The wait removes urgency and self-sourcing — buys time for a critical objection to surface. The wait is the point.

RED → FLOOR → WAY → BROTHERS → GOD · halt at first failure · no consensus override

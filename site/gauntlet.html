The Fabrication Gauntlet

Every AI answer is, in the end, “trust me.” This one hands you a receipt.

Each claim below is the kind of thing you might be told. The engine doesn't guess — it checks the math underneath, returns a verdict, shows the worked trail, and mints a permanent, tamper-evident seal you can re-check yourself. A false claim is refuted as citably as a true one is confirmed.

We built this expecting to catch LLMs failing. Then we tested honestly — and a leading model flagged every one of these correctly. That is the point. A smart model is usually right, on its word, with no proof and no guarantee. The engine is right every time, shows its work, and gives you a seal anyone can re-check — and it has never confirmed a falsehood across its benchmark. Verification isn't about catching a dumb model. It's about not having to trust a clever one.

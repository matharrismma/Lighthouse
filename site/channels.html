Channels

The channel is the funnel; the site is the home. Narrow Highway runs one 24/7 broadcast channel — hymns in the morning, sci-fi at primetime, Saturday cartoons → matinee → roller derby → night movie, Sunday hymns and sermons all day. The channel exists to bring families to the curated internet, where the channel is one of many decks alongside the Codex, the Almanac, the Walk, the Apothecary, Kids storytime, Games, Tools, Scribe, Journal, and more.

Distribution targets for the channel: Roku, Plex Live, YouTube Live, Pluto, Samsung TV+, LG Channels — wherever it can be carried. Every viewer who finds us via the channel finds a home at narrowhighway.com.

The channel is one tool

Narrow Highway is a portfolio. The channel is one tool — a funnel that pulls families in via Roku, Plex, YouTube Live, etc. Other tools in the portfolio: Codex · Homeschool curriculum · Almanac · Apothecary · Walk · Hymns · Kids · Games · Tools · Scribe · Journal · Submit · Support. Each follows the same model: curated substrate, alignment gate, community feedback, never extractive.

If you'd like to be on it

If you make family-safe work — music, theatre, sport, comedy, sermons, kids' content — and want it on the schedule, email partnerships@narrowhighway.com. The schedule fills with what we have on hand; when something better arrives and clears review, the schedule changes. No partnerships, no platform deals, no distribution promises — just an invitation.

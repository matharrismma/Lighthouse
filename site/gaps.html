What the engine can and cannot verify

Gaps.

The engine confirms only what closes from math against constants of truth. Authority lookups don't count. If the math doesn't run, the claim doesn't go on the site. This page is the operational expression of that principle: what we can verify lives in the verifier catalog; what we can't yet lives here, with the math chain each gap would need.

# Lighthouse Canon (Summary)

**Authority Order**
1. Jesus' words (RED)
2. Moral floor (Law)
3. The Way (wisdom through obedience)

**Four Gates**
- RED → FLOOR → BROTHERS → GOD

**Properties**
- Minimal
- Fractal
- Forkable
- Non-coercive
- Self-pruning

This canon governs decisions without requiring rulers.

Develop the Cards

Every card, by how finished it is — so the corpus coheres instead of just accumulating

A card isn't done until it's developed — a substantive body (300+ chars) and at least two connections and a passed witness. Anything less is still in progress. This board shows the whole content corpus against that definition of done, so the work is visible and finishable — not a pile that only grows.

Click a stage to see its cards. Each links to the card to develop it. Connections are the biggest lever — most cards have a body but too few links.
